This is where you can understand how to use our ticket system. Below is an overview of the ticket system. and what it looks like when you have a ticket open.

![Tickets](/media/billing-ticket.png)

## How to Create a Ticket

1. Click on the **Open Ticket** Button.
2. Fill out the form with the required information, Below is a screenshot of what the ticket form looks like.
4. Click the **Open Ticket**  Button.


![Tickets](/media/billing-ticket-open.png)


## Ticket Chat
This is where you can send messages to the support team and they can respond to you, Add attachments and more. Below is a screenshot of what the ticket chat looks like with a reply. 
![Tickets](/media/ticket-chat2.png)

## Closing a Ticket

To close the ticket you can either:
1. Click the dropdown and click the **Close** button.
2. Click on the ticket chat and click the **Close** button.

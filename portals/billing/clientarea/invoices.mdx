# How to Pay an Invoice

Click on the view button to view the invoice. Once you are on the invoice, select the way you would like to pay the invoice. Then click the *Pay* Button.

The pay option should be removed and the text at the top right should say Invoice Paid. Below is a screenshot of what the invoice page looks like when you are paying an invoice.
![Invoices](/media/pay-invoice.png)

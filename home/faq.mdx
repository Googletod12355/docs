## General Questions

**Q: I haven't received my email. What should I do?**
A: Please wait 10 minutes before opening a support ticket.

**Q: Are the packages a one-time payment or monthly?**
A: All packages have a monthly cost currently but may change to weekly or yearly in the future.

**Q: I created an order but haven't received the server on the panel. What's going on?**
A: Ensure you've completed the payment for the invoice to receive your product.

**Q: Why do we use paymenter?**
A: We use paymenter because we feel its better for our clients to use, has a cleaner interface and is easier to use.

**Q: Why are you seperating free & Paid clients?**
A: We separate free and paid clients to ensure that paid clients have a better experience. We still try to ensure the experience is equal for both free and paid clients but paid clients have separate improved systems to make our life easier.

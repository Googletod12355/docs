### Our Nodes  
We currently operate three nodes, strategically located in the United Kingdom, United States and Germany to provide low-latency performance

### Our Panel  
To ensure seamless performance and minimize downtime, our control panel runs on a **VPS**, separate from the hosting infrastructure. This allows for faster response times and uninterrupted management capabilities.
